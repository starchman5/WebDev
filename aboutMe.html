Just a kid with a dream

I am Travis from Orange County. Rock N' Roll is near-religious for me. I'm a guitarist, a luthier, a sporadic bagpiper (seriously) and I like to cook, take drives while listening to music, and play with my crazy dogs. I would like to get a project car or two sometime in the future and begin a restoration. I have been working as a pharmacy technician for several years since graduating from Arizona State University. I began my journey here by taking some computer science courses at Pasadena City College.

Some of the cars I would choose for my restoration project are (in no particular order):

  • 1969 Chevy Chevelle
  • 1978 Chevy El Camino
  • 1957 Chevy Bel Air
  • 1966 Ford Mustang
  • 1955 Cadillac DeVille

My All-Time Top 5 albums would be as follows:
